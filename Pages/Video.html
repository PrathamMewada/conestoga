Bestseller of all time

"Nuclear War: A Scenario" by Annie Jacobsen

This is the bestselling modern novel, part of a series with over 500 million total sales worldwide.

"The Lord of the Rings" by J.R.R. Tolkien

This three volume work of epic fantasy was released in 1954 and seems to have passed 150 million copies. Its well-developed setting, bright personalities, and humorous, swift, and moralistic paired with the show of power between good and evil has turned the novel into the classic.

And Then There Were None by Agatha Christie

Often cited as the world's most successful mystery story, this detective novel, written in 1939, has been bought 100 million times. Especially praised for its keeping up of intricate mysteries, the plot is based on inviting ten people to a mysterious island where each of them they die one by one.
